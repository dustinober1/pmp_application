## 2026 Mindset: Justify & Authorize

### Key Questions at Initiation
*   **Why are we doing this project?** (Business Case)
*   **Who is in charge and what are they authorized to do?** (Project Charter)
*   **How will we deliver this project?** (Delivery Strategy)

### Core Takeaways
*   **Value First**: Every project must tie back to business value or a strategic objective.
*   **Formal Authorization**: The Project Charter is not optional; it's the PM's license to operate.
*   **Adaptive Context**: Choose your approach (Agile, Hybrid, Predictive) deliberately, don't default.
